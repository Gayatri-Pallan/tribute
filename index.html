Dr.A.P.J Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

Missle Man of India

Dr.Aval Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdual Kalam BR was an Indian aerospace scientist and statement who served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born and raise in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu and studied physics and aerospace engineering. Under his Leadership, India achieved remarkable breakthroughs in missile technology, including successfully testing the Agni and Prithvi missiles. In 2002, Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was elected as the 11th President of India.A deep commitment to eduaction, youth, and technology characterized his presidency.
